What is VacSEEN?

With US pioneering certain freedom to vaccinated individuals, vaccine card checking is implemented. But, this takes time to verify especially if there are a lot of customers and not a lot manpower. VacSEEN makes vaccine card checking shorter by giving you a reliable, secure, and easy to use QR code that you can use from your phone or in print.

Benefits of Using VacSEEN

Employees

Get hired faster because lets you enter the physical workspace without having to interact with the vaccine card checking attendee.

Employers

Avoid congestion in the checking area and avoid counterfeit vaccine card.

Personal

Walk inside malls, restaurants, and hotels faster.

What VacSEEN is NOT

VacSEEN will NOT give unvaccinated individuals a vaccine pass

Who can use VacSEEN?

Vaccinated Individuals, Employers and, Private & Government Institutions who want to implement this system.
